Introducing Holographic Language for AI

Human languages were designed for conversation — not computation. They're riddled with ambiguity, context-dependence, and words that shift meaning mid-sentence. When we force AI to reason by predicting the next word, we inherit all of that messiness. The Holographic Language Framework proposes a different substrate: a mathematically precise vector algebra where every operation is exact, every retrieval is instant, and hallucination has nowhere to hide.
