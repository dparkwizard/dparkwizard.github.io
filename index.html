Thermodynamics Channel Capacity Saturation

The "Billion Token Fallacy" assumes the Context Window acts as lossless memory. In reality, the Attention Mechanism is a Competitive Interference Channel.

Every distractor token adds to the normalization constant ($Z$). As $N \to \infty$, the cumulative thermodynamic noise mathematically drowns out the specific target signal unless the model's dimensional resolution can outpace it logarithmically.

$$\Delta E = E_{\text{noise}} - E_{\text{signal}} > \ln(N)$$

Latent Space Confabulation as Relaxation

When $Z$ explodes, the prompt's local "Context Valley" becomes washed out by high-entropy noise. The model's latent state naturally rolls out of the shallow context and settles into the deep Prior Canyon (its pre-trained defaults). Hallucination is merely thermodynamic relaxation to the mean.

$$\hat{y} = \arg\max P(y) $$
